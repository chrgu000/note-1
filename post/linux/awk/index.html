1. AWK

1.1. 去除重复行

awk '!a[$0]++' test-ins-2.txt
cat arg.txt | awk -F '.' '{if($NF ~ "java"){print $0}}' | xargs -I t dirname t | awk '!a[$0]++' | xargs -I s ls s | awk '{print $1}' | awk -F'.' '{if($1 ~ /\$/){print $1}}'

1.2. ps 查询进程

ps -ef|grep varnish | awk '{if($1 ~ "varnish"){print $2}}' | xargs sudo kill -9
